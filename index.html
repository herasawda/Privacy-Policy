Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information unless you choose to log in to access the chat and profile features. If you decide to use these features, we may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information, which includes:

  • Phone number for OTP (One-Time Password) based login authentication
  • Access to device gallery for updating profile images
  • Access to device camera for capturing and updating profile images

This information is collected and used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the chat and profile features. You can use the basic features of the app without logging in or providing any personal information.

The phone number is collected and used solely for the purpose of authentication and securing your account. Gallery and camera access permissions are requested only when you choose to update your profile image, and these permissions can be revoked through your device settings at any time.

The app does utilize third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Third-party service providers include:

  • Google Play Services

We use AppsFlyer for app analytics with privacy protection. We do not collect advertising identifiers, device IDs, or other personal device information from any users.

Account Deletion and Data Removal

If you have created an account to use the chat and profile features, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. When you choose to delete your account:

  • All your personal information will be permanently deleted from our databases
  • Your profile information and any uploaded content will be removed from our servers
  • Your account credentials and authentication data will be permanently erased

To delete your account, you can use the account deletion option in the app settings. Once initiated, the deletion process cannot be reversed, and all data will be permanently removed from our systems.
